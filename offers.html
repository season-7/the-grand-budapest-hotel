Offers

Whether travelling for business or pleasure this five-star East-African hotel offers luxurious accommodation with modern amenities as well as the joy of staying in an impeccably restored heritage building. The Grand Budapest Hotel packages offer excellent value for a memorable stay in one of the capital's most charming hotels.

Family Package

Discover East Africa with your family this year. With interconnecting and adjoining suite and room combinations, The Grand Budapest is the ideal home away from home for your family. Children are welcomed with bespoke amenities, activities, games and more. Relax and unwind after a busy day in Sense Spa's sauna and steam room followed by a well-deserved night's sleep in your urban sanctuary.

What is included:

  • Two rooms or Suite with second bedroom.
  • Full English Breakfast for two adults and two children (up to 16 years old).
  • Rose Buds Children Programme - age appropriate children amenities and activities.
  • Selection of games consoles.
  • Opportunity to meet Pearl, the hotel dog.
  • Complimentary access to Sense Spa's sauna and steam room.
  • Complimentary WiFi
  • 24HR Gym Access
  • Guaranteed late check out until 3pm

Spa Experience Package

Experience a rejuvenating spa break with The Grand Budapest by enjoying a one night stay in your choice of room or suite, including English breakfast, and a one hour treatment at Sense Spa – the only spa in East Africa to offer an array of nurturing treatments using Sodashi products to relax and soothe. The spa's zen theme features bamboo walls, soft lighting and wooden walkways over rippling water and pebble stones whilst facilities include dry heat saunas, amethyst crystal steam rooms and a shimmering gold leaf and teak relaxation lounge providing a restorative hideaway in the heart of East Africa.

What is included:

  • Overnight accommodation.
  • A one hour treatment of your choice from the Spa menu for 2 people.
  • Full English breakfast for two.
  • Complimentary access to sense Spa's sauna and steam room.
  • Complimentary WiFi.
  • 24HR Gym access.
